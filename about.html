The problem

Most directories sell rankings to the highest bidder. Iranian diaspora clients — navigating immigration, family law across borders, sanctions, inheritance — need to find a lawyer they can trust, not the one who paid the most.

We built this because it didn't exist.

What we are

A free, community-run directory of Iranian lawyers worldwide. Every listing is verified against the relevant regulator's public register before it appears. No fees. No tiers. No bought rankings.

What we are not

We are not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice. We do not recommend or endorse individual lawyers. We do not accept reviews or paid placements. Engaging a lawyer is your own decision.

Who runs it

Volunteers from the Iranian diaspora community. No agency. No marketing company. No hidden owner. Run for the community, by the community. If it ever stops being that, it should stop existing.
