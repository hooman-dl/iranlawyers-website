Principles

Our Promise

Four rules. No exceptions.

These aren't marketing promises. They are the rules this directory runs on. If we ever break them, the directory should stop existing.
01

Free, forever

No listing fees. No “Gold” or “Platinum” tiers. No 90-day trial that quietly downgrades you. If you're a verified Iranian lawyer, you're listed for as long as you want to be.

02

No paid placements

Nobody pays for a higher position. Listings are sorted by location and recency, not by who wrote the biggest cheque this month. The order is the same for everyone.

03

Verification means something

Before publishing, we sight your practising certificate and confirm it against the regulator's public register. Every profile shows the verification date and the regulator's name.

04

No hidden pricing

Because there is no pricing. No agency owns this. No marketing company sells SEO packages off the back of it. Run by volunteers as a community resource.
